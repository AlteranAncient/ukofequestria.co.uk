We've moved to Discord!

Due to increasing time and financial costs that we are no longer able to commit to, we have made the tough decision to close down the forums and exist exclusively on Discord.

It is much harder now to drive traffic towards bespoke forum websites, in a world quite different from the one we had ten years ago where search engines and word-of-mouth were your primary sources of new members.

We would have loved to maintain a legacy with messages accessible in a read-only format, but time, financial and technical constraints combined with the need to respect people's data privacy, makes this a bit too difficult for us.

Thanks for sticking with us for the past decade! We hope you'll join us in our Discord server.
